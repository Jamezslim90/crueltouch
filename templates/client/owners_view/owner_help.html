General

What functionality I implemented ?
For the administrator, you can login in 2 ways, through the admin page and through the super admin page, which you can access through the settings in the left panel. When you log in as admin here, you get a preview in the dashboard of how many photos you have uploaded to your web server for your clients. This helps you know when to delete old images and therefore have more space for further uploads, as you only have a total of 9 GB space in your VPS. You can also see how many customers have signed up on your website, how many contact forms (or messages) you have in total, and the total number of sessions (photo shoot) you have completed.
To be implemented
When you click on the name of a client, you will be able to upload several photos for him at the same time, see all the information relating to this client, how many reservations he has made, information relating to these reservations (done, pending, accepted, canceled), see all his unretouched photos, see his zip file containing all the photos. You will be able to modify the information related to this customer like his name, his e-mail address etc ... Rest assured, you can already do all this in the super admin page, but I admit that it is not user-friendly.

Recent Booking
A list of all your recent reservations.
Status of a booking
Pending : The client went through your website and made a reservation for a session with you but it's not yet accepted
Accepted : If you accept the conditions of the reservation, you will change the status to accepted.
Paid (to be implemented) : The client has done a first deposit via paypal through the website or via any other platform (if so you will change the status from accepted to paid)
Done : All the conditions were accepted, you received your fist deposit, you have done the photo session and edited all the photos that the client downloaded and you received your final payments.
Canceled : Unfortunately, the client canceled the photo session.

Password

Change or modify password
The website does not store raw (clear text) passwords on the user model, but only a hash. Because of this, do not attempt to manipulate the password attribute of the user directly. This is why a helper function is used when creating a user.
